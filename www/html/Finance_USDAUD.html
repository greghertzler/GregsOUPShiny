Finance_USDAUD {GregsOUPR6}R Documentation

US Dollars per Australian Dollar

Description

Daily exchange rates from 1 July 2024 to 30 June 2025, with AUD as the base currency and USD as the quote currency

Format

csv file with 258 rows and 7 columns

Details

  • Day: time variable in days since 1 July 2024

  • Open: Rate at session open in USD/AUD

  • High: Daily high rate in USD/AUD

  • Low: Daily low rate in USD/AUD

  • Close: Rate at session close in USD/AUD

  • Adj Close: Same as Close for exchange rates

  • Year: time variable as decimal year

Source

https://finance.yahoo.com/quote (search for AUDUSD=X)
