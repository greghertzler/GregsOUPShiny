Finance_KansasCity_WheatFutures {GregsOUPR6}R Documentation

Kansas City Hard Red Wheat Futures

Description

Daily futures for September 2025 maturity from 1 July 2024 to 30 June 2025

Format

csv file with 250 rows and 8 columns

Details

  • Day: time variable in days since 1 July 2024

  • Open: Price at market open in USD/ton

  • High: Daily high price in USD/ton

  • Low: Daily low price in USD/ton

  • Close: Price at market close in USD/ton

  • Adj Close: Same as Close for futures

  • Volume: Daily number of trades

  • Year: time variable as decimal year

Source

https://finance.yahoo.com/quote (search for KE=F)
