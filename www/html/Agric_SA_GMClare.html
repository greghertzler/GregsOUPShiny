Agric_SA_GMClare {GregsOUPR6}R Documentation

Farm Adaptation at Clare, South Australia

Description

APSIM generated Wheat yields and manually simulated dry-sheep equivalents

Format

csv file with 108 rows and 7 columns

Details

  • Year: time variable in annual increments

  • Annual rain: Annual rainfall in millimetres

  • Apr-Oct rain: Growing season rainfall in millimetres

  • Wheat Yld: Wheat yield in tonnes per hectare

  • Sheep DSE: Dry-sheep equivalents per hectare

  • Wheat GM: Wheat gross margins per hectare

  • Sheep GM: Sheep gross margins per hectare

Source

https://nccarf.edu.au/will-primary-producers-continue-adjust-practices-and-technologies-change-production/
