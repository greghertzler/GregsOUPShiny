Agric_NSW_SoilHealthHarden {GregsOUPR6}R Documentation

Soil Health with Stubble Management

Description

CSIRO Harden Long-Term Tillage Experiment

Format

csv file with 30 rows and 5 columns

Details

  • Year: time variable in sporadic increments

  • N Burn: kilograms per hectare of mineral soil nitrogen in the soil profile from 0 to 160 millimetres with stubble burning

  • N Bash: kilograms per hectare of mineral soil nitrogen in the soil profile from 0 to 160 millimetres with stubble bashing

  • W Burn: millimetres of water in the soil profile from 0 to 160 millimetres with stubble burning

  • W Bash: millimetres of water in the soil profile from 0 to 160 millimetres with stubble bashing

Source

https://data.csiro.au/collection/csiro:61293?q=population%20data&_st=keyword&_str=210&_si=7
