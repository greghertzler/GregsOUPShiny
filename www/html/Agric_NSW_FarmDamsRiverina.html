Agric_NSW_FarmDamsRiverina {GregsOUPR6}R Documentation

Water in Farm Dams in the Riverina of New South Wales

Description

Matlab generated water volumes in a 4000 cubic metre dam, with and without tree windbreaks to control evaporation, and a merino sheep flock size of 1500 dry sheep equivalents.

Format

csv file with 19313 rows and 3 columns

Details

  • Day: time variable in daily increments

  • Without: water volumes in cubic metres without a windbreak

  • With: water volumes in cubic metres with a windbreak
